# Code of Conduct

Our shared values define how we work together.

## Respect

Treat every colleague with dignity. Disagreement is welcome; disrespect is not.

## Integrity

Be honest about your work. If something is broken, say so. If you don't know, ask.

## Inclusion

We do our best work when everyone is heard. Create space for quieter voices in meetings; recognize contributions publicly.

## Reporting Concerns

If you witness or experience a violation, contact HR. All reports are confidential and will be investigated promptly.
